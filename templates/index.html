Meet the Owner

Hi, I'm Chauhan Anand Singh 👋
Your Local Stationery Friend

"I started Genius Point because I remember how hard it was to find good quality stationery at fair prices near our area. I wanted to change that for every student in Nava Naroda."

After serving the nation in the Indian Army’s paramilitary forces and receiving multiple awards for his service, he now continues his journey by serving the local community. With discipline, honesty, and care, he has built this store to support students, families, and everyday needs.

Every product is personally selected, and every customer is treated with trust. Whether it’s school supplies, bulk orders, or urgent printing, just visit or message on WhatsApp — you’ll always get reliable help.
